Dress Code

Guests are encouraged to wear rich earth-toned traditional attire. The couple's families will be in Royal Blue and Coral respectively.

Spraying Policy

In accordance with tradition, moderate spraying is permitted. Designated "Moneyminders" will assist in collecting and securing offerings.

Cultural Note

The "Ido-Oba" is a time for the groom to demonstrate humility. Prostrating before the elders is a key sign of respect.
